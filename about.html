About Me

I am an experienced software engineer, with B.Sc, in Computer science, working in Israeli Hi-tech for about 20 years.

For more information about my professional experience, please visit my LinkedIN profile

I know how to translate the competitive nature of sports into life routine in general and career goals in particular.
I like traveling the world and studying different cultures. I like visiting restaurants and eating delicious food.
As an athlete my first activity was running. It soon became long distance running and then I ended running marathons and participating in public events and then in competitive races. That is how I discovered I liked racing and overall being competitive. After few years of running I discovered another sport - cycling.
At first this was on a cheap mountain bike, riding with friends. Soon I shifted to road bike and joined a new competitive cycling team, IGP, which just emerged. As of now, after 15 years in IGP, I train 4-6 times a week and participate in many competitive races.
As a bonus, each year, around mid-July, the team travels to famous cycling sites places in Europe, such as the Dolomites or Alps at Italy. I consider these trips as genuine vacations - spending time with old and new friends on good food and wine, visiting beautiful famous places, and of course - cycling.
